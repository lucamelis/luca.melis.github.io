About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at University College London, where I work under the supervision of Dr. Emiliano De Cristofaro and Dr. George Danezis. My research interests are in the fields of applied cryptography, privacy and cloud security.

Before joining UCL, I received my BSc. and MSc. in Computer Engineering from the University of Florence.

More details about me can be found in my curriculum vitae.
